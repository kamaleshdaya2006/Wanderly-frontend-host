Marina Beach

Sandy toes, sun-kissed nose!

Queen of Chennai’s Coastline

Marina Beach is the pride of Chennai and one of the longest urban beaches in the world. Stretching along the Bay of Bengal, it is a vibrant destination where golden sands meet the endless horizon. From sunrise walks and kite flying to local street food and cultural landmarks, Marina offers a perfect blend of relaxation and city life.

Famous Street Foods at Marina

Taste the flavors that define the Marina evenings.

Sundal

★★★★★ 4.8

A classic beach snack made from boiled chickpeas mixed with coconut, raw mango, and spices. Light, tangy, and refreshing after a walk by the sea. Popular at: Raja Sundal Stall.

Murukku & Chips

★★★★☆ 4.5

Crunchy, spicy, and addictive. Vendors sell fresh murukku, potato chips, and masala mixtures that are perfect for munching while watching the waves. Famous at: Beachside Snack Carts.

Bajji

★★★★★ 4.7

Deep-fried banana, chilli, or potato slices coated in gram flour batter. Served hot with coconut chutney. Must-try at: Murugan Bajji Stall.

Masala Corn

★★★★☆ 4.4

Steamed corn tossed with butter, chilli powder, lemon juice, and salt. A warm, spicy snack loved by evening visitors. Popular at: Marina Corn Point.

Souvenirs to Take Home

Small treasures that carry the spirit of Marina.

Seashell Crafts

★★★★☆ 4.5

Handcrafted shell necklaces, lamps, and decorative pieces made by local artisans. Popular at Marina Shell Corner.

Wooden Toys

★★★★☆ 4.4

Colorful handmade toys and miniature carts inspired by traditional South Indian designs. Found at Beachside Craft Stalls.

Handmade Keychains

★★★★☆ 4.3

Affordable and vibrant keychains shaped like boats, shells, and Chennai landmarks. Available at Sunset Souvenir Booths.

Beach Hats & Accessories

★★★★☆ 4.2

Straw hats, sunglasses, and beachwear perfect for sunny walks along the coastline. Popular at Marina Fashion Stalls.
